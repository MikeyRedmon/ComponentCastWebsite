    Our Mission Statement

    We strive to produce the highest quality TTRPG content avalible, while providing space for new and upcoming creators in the space to be creative knowing their ideas and passions will be met with encouragment and support

    About Us

    The Component Cast was started by our Executive Producer, Alex "Midgeman" Dunk. He along with the rest of the team have a massive passion for TTRPGs. Alex, along with several founding members began the first show to a roaring two hundred viewers at peak. Ever since we have been broadcasting various TTRPGs including Dungeons and Dragons 5e, Cyberpunk Red, Urban Jungle and Zweihander to name a few.

    Our Promise

    We Promise to provide an inclusive, welcoming community that will support any and all creators on the shows to the fullest of their abilities. Allowing them to grow, meet and exceed their potiential.
